# Doripe B2B Blog Article Template

## Metadata

- Title:
- Primary keyword:
- Secondary keywords:
- Cluster:
- Search intent:
- Target space types:
- CTA strength: light
- Article-specific operator scenario:
- Source/evidence notes:

Before drafting, write the operator scenario in 2-4 concrete sentences. It must name the space type, current channel or touchpoint, observed symptom, and the business tension.

Acceptable scenario example:

> A 12-seat wine bar in a side street gets good reactions from regulars, but new visitors only come after being brought by friends. The Instagram feed has bottle photos and reservation notices, while Naver shows accurate hours but little about mood or who the bar is comfortable for. The operator wants more first-time visits without discount events.

Do not draft from a generic scenario such as "a local store needs more customers."

## 1. Problem Hook

Start from the operator's concrete problem. Use plain Korean and avoid product language.

Example:

> 손님이 완전히 없는 것은 아닌데, 새로 오는 사람이 늘지 않는다면 노출보다 먼저 점검해야 할 것이 있습니다.

The hook must reflect the article-specific operator scenario. Avoid opening with a general marketing claim that could fit any article.

## 2. Why This Happens

Explain the underlying reason without blaming the operator.

Cover one or more of:

- The space is visible but not memorable.
- Photos show the menu but not the visit situation.
- Map information answers facts but not desire.
- Instagram posts look nice but do not create a visit candidate.
- Reviews exist but do not explain who the space is for.

Tie the explanation to a real touchpoint when relevant:

- Naver Place / map search
- Instagram feed, Reels, Stories, pinned posts, or profile
- Review replies and review prompts
- Reservation, DM, phone, form, or walk-in path
- In-store signage, table cards, menus, or event notices

## 3. Practical Checklist

Give 5-7 concrete checks the operator can apply today.

Each checklist item should include:

- What to check.
- Why it matters.
- A small example.

When the article involves a channel or action path, at least 3 checklist items must include concrete steps. Name the exact place to change something, the action to take, and the expected reader/customer effect.

Example:

> Instagram profile: replace a vague one-line intro with "quiet 10-seat dessert bar for after-dinner coffee, Fri-Sun until midnight." This helps a first-time visitor decide whether the space fits the occasion before opening the feed.

## 4. Before / After Example

Include one before/after example using the article's scenario.

The before/after must show a specific improvement, not a broad principle.

Use this shape:

- Before: what the operator currently says, shows, or does.
- After: the revised message, content order, review prompt, map field, signage, or follow-up.
- Why it works: one sentence tied to saves, visit confidence, atmosphere, repeat visits, or community memory.

Example:

- Before: "New menu launched."
- After: "A late-night pear tart for people who want one quiet dessert after dinner. Available from 7 p.m.; 8 seats only."
- Why it works: it gives the visitor a situation, time, and reason to save the space instead of treating the post as a menu notice.

## 5. Examples By Space Type

Include at least 2 examples from different space types when the topic allows.

Possible types:

- Restaurant
- Cafe
- Bar
- Shop
- Bookstore
- Studio
- Gallery
- Pop-up

## 6. Evidence / Source Notes

Add either evidence/source notes or explicit field observations.

Use one of these options:

- Public source notes: cite the platform field, public review pattern, search result behavior, or published operator/customer observation used to shape the advice.
- Field observations: state what a writer observed from local space accounts, maps, reviews, menus, signage, booking flows, or visit-path friction.
- Doripe observations: for Doripe report/case articles, explain the observed pattern without claiming statistical proof unless a real dataset is available.

Do not invent numbers, survey results, rankings, or ROI claims. If the article is based on qualitative observation, say so plainly.

## 7. Common Mistakes

List 3-5 mistakes.

Avoid generic advice like "post consistently" unless it is tied to a specific operator action.

## 8. Doripe Perspective

Add a short section that connects the topic to Doripe's view of local discovery.

Use this tone:

> Doripe는 공간을 단순한 광고 지면이 아니라, 손님이 저장하고 다시 꺼내볼 수 있는 방문 후보로 바라봅니다.

Do not paste the same Doripe sentence in every article. Adapt the perspective to the cluster:

- Reviews / saves / visit conversion: saved spaces need enough confidence to become an actual visit.
- Space branding / atmosphere: the mood of a space should be remembered as a visit situation, not only as decoration.
- Repeat / regulars / community: the best local discovery continues after the first visit.
- Doripe reports / cases: use Doripe as an observation lens, not as a proof machine.

## 9. Soft CTA

Use a light or medium CTA only. Do not overpromise.

Choose and adapt one CTA variant. Do not copy the same sentence into every article.

CTA variants:

- Light / pilot interest: "Doripe는 작은 공간을 취향 기반 카드로 소개하는 파일럿을 준비하고 있습니다. 우리 공간이 어떤 장면으로 보일 수 있을지 궁금하다면 파일럿 소식을 받아보세요."
- Light / checklist continuation: "공간의 첫인상, 저장 이유, 방문 전 정보를 함께 점검하고 싶다면 Doripe 파일럿 업데이트를 확인해보세요."
- Medium / operator fit: "우리 공간이 저장되고 다시 떠오르는 방식으로 소개될 수 있을지 보고 싶다면 Doripe 파일럿 신청을 남겨주세요."

## 10. Publish Acceptance Criteria

Do not publish unless every item below is true:

- The draft starts from an article-specific operator scenario, not a generic marketing problem.
- The article includes one before/after example tied to the scenario.
- Channel/action advice names the concrete place and step where relevant.
- Evidence/source notes or field observations are included and do not invent unsupported numbers.
- At least two space types appear when the topic allows.
- Doripe appears as a light perspective, not a hard sell.
- The CTA is adapted from one variant and matches the assigned CTA strength.
- The article avoids top-ranking, guaranteed sales, and ROI promises.
